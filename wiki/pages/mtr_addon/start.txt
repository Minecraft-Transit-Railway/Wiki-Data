====== MTR Addons ======

MTR Addons are Minecraft Mods that add new content and functionality (Such as blocks or features) that integrates with the MTR Mod. They are installed like a regular Minecraft Mod, with the exception that they require MTR Mod to be installed as well.

===== List of MTR Addons =====

==== Joban Client Mod ====

[[mtr_addon:jcm:start|Joban Client Mod]] is an addon based on Minecraft Transit Railway Mod, adding various blocks from the Hong Kong MTR and utility blocks that will greatly improve your world. Some of the blocks this mod adds including Static Signal Light, Fare Saver machine, Railway Vision PIDS and other decoration blocks to help you build your very own railway system!

==== Reality City Construction ====

[[mtr_addon:rcc:start|Reality City Construction]] is a module created by Yaoxiansen and MSnj, named after Yaoxiansen. After a year of production, the module has been basically improved.

The module adds blocks for lines and urban construction, tactile paving, platform blocks, etc. In the future, domestic subway facilities such as gates and security checkpoints will also be added.

<WRAP round download 90%>

**Download** \\  <wrap safety round box>MTR 3</wrap> [[https://modrinth.com/mod/reality-c-c]]

</WRAP>

==== The Tube (aka: London Underground) ====

[[mtr_addon:lu:start|The Tube addon]] provides blocks to create Tunnels and Stations and station signage. Other great additions include a London Underground themed Platform Information Display and a red/green tunnel signal! There are also some great ambient sound effects to bring your tube stations to life!

'''Let's Play Season 1 Finale''' is the last episode of Season 1 in the [[mtr_lp:let_s_play|Let's Play Series]], filmed on 24 September, 2022 and published on on 25 September.

This is the longest video in the Let's Play series, containing [[mtr_lp:spawn_station|Spawn]] renovation and numerous tours on the new lines, such as [[mtr_lp:wlt_line_1|WLT Line 1]], [[mtr_lp:azalea_cyan_line|Azalea Cyan Line]], [[mtr_lp:shinx_line|Shinx Line]], [[mtr_lp:wr_yanyu_line|WR Yanyu Line]], [[mtr_lp:northern_circle_line|Northern Circle Line]] and [[mtr_lp:prison_line|Prison Line]].

Due to a relatively large number of HK based players in discord voice chat, Cantonese is also spoken beside English.

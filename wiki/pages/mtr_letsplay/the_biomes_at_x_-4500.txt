====== The Biomes at X -4500 Area ======

**The Biomes at X -4500 Area**, (Chinese: //X軸負四千五百生態群集區//), is a narrow and long strip of diverse biomes along the [[/wlt_line_1|Line 1 (Meyan Line)]] starting from Tung Yuen station until the river north of Chi Chuk station. It separates CinderaceOTS's area at the west and Denthrope area together with Northern Snowland at the east.

=====Lilac Light Rail Route 492, 483P=====
**Lilac Light Rail Route 492** (Chinese: **麗蓮輕鐵492綫**) is a Lilac Light Rail route connecting Bridge Bay stop and Jonathan’s Resort stop, and is jointly owned by Lilypad Rail and Qingli Railways.

**Lilac Light Rail Route 483P** (Chinese: **麗蓮輕鐵483P綫**) is a longer version of route 492, connecting Chou Wai and Light Rail Depot.

====History====
On 6 February 2023, Lilypad Rail proposed a new route 492 starting from Jonathan’s Resort to Bridge Bay. This is the first light rail route serving the west of Jonathan’s Resort. On the same day, Qingli Railways also announced to cooperate with Lilypad Rail to operate a new peak hour light rail route 492P running from Chou Wai to Light Rail Depot.

On 10 February 2023, the track section between Jonathan’s Resort and Chou Wai was completed. Route 492 and 492P went fully operational to Chou Wai. The route number of 492P has been converted to 483P.

On 18 February 2023, the track section between Bridge Bay and Chou Wai is completed. Route 492 is extended to Bridge Bay stop, but Route 483P is not.

On 29 October 2023, Royal Park is added to Chou Wai bound of route 483P for future connections to Elizabeth line and [[lrt_804|route 804]].

According to "Lilac Light Rail Reform Plan 2023", Routes 492 and 483P will be unchanged, but the frequencies will be lowered to 5 and 15 minutes respectively.

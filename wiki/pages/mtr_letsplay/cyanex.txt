===== Hierarchy =====

**CyanEX Rapid Transit** is a subsidiary of the **CyanEX Holdings Group**. 

===== History =====

CyanEX was initially established in December 2021 at the [[https://xinghaicity.ldiorstudio.cn|XinghaiCity (StarRiverCity)]] community server, with its initial name being BluExp. It was renamed to CyanEX in March 2022 following Asnacinjar's join. Asnacinjar coined the current English name and designed the current logo.

CyanEX (under the older name "CyanEX Mobility") operated various urban and suburban railways services in XinghaiCity.

  * [[https://xinghaicity.ldiorstudio.cn/wiki/%E5%86%AC%E5%A5%A5%E7%8E%AF%E7%BA%BF|Winter Olympics Loop Line]]
  * Autumn Valley Line (with the [[https://xinghaicity.ldiorstudio.cn/wiki/%E6%A9%99%E8%B0%B7%E7%BA%BF%E5%8D%97%E5%8C%97%E7%9B%B4%E9%80%9A%E5%BF%AB%E9%80%9F|North-South Through Express Service]])
  * [[https://xinghaicity.ldiorstudio.cn/wiki/%E8%93%9D%E6%98%9F%E4%B8%9C%E8%A5%BF%E7%BA%BF|CyanEX Tozai Line]]
  * [[https://xinghaicity.ldiorstudio.cn/wiki/%E5%8D%9A%E7%89%A9%E9%A6%86APM|Transport Museum APM]]
  * [[https://xinghaicity.ldiorstudio.cn/wiki/%E5%8C%97%E5%A2%83%E7%BA%BF|Hokukyu Line]]
  * [[https://xinghaicity.ldiorstudio.cn/wiki/%E5%8C%97%E7%99%BD%E7%BA%BF|Hokukyo-Snowwhite Line]]
  * [[https://xinghaicity.ldiorstudio.cn/wiki/%E5%8C%97%E5%AE%89%E7%BA%BF|Hokukyo-Anting Line]]
  * [[https://xinghaicity.ldiorstudio.cn/wiki/14%E5%8F%B7%E7%BA%BF|Line 14]]

The Winter Olympics Loop Line was once the longest operating line in Xinghai City.

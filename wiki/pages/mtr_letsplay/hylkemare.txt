====== Hylkemare Station ======
**Hylkemare** is a overground railway terminal with 9 platforms, serving Moszyan Railways' overground service system. The station later started serving the Lilac Light Rail, via an external tram and bus station called **Hylkemare Station**. In 2026, the east side of the station was taken into use, starting with lines to **Spawn**, **Yunlong** and **Yankton**.

===== Layout =====
The main station has 9 platforms, 2 facing west and the remaining 7 facing east. //Fryske Line// services depart from the west side of the station, while the remaining services depart from the east-facing platforms. Originally, the //Fryske Line// was the only line to serve this station, until further developments in the east saw the opening of the //Anemone Line// and //Intercity// services to **Spawn** and **Yunlong**. A few weeks later, the //KTT// was also extended from **Kallos LRT Depot** to follow the //Intercity// down to **Hylkemare**.

To the east of the main entrance is the tram and bus station, serving a municipal bus service based on the //Boskoop Buurtbus//, and a number of //Lilac Light Rail// services. Any trains that cannot turn around at the middle platform continue to the loop at **Hylkemare Oostbaai**.

===== Trivia =====
  * As of January 30th 2026, **Hylkemare** is available as a warp in the Donator's Lobby. The warp spawns you on the sidewalk between the front entrance and the light rail tracks, just in front of the zebra crossing.
  * **Hylkemare** was built to look like the dutch **Leeuwarden** station, but a mistake by Szandor ended in it having two unintended extra terminus platforms.
  * The name **Hylkemare** is a very simple derivative of the Frisian surname "Hylkema".

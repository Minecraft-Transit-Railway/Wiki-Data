======Lilac Light Rail Route 810======
**Lilac Light Rail Route 810** (Chinese: **麗蓮輕鐵810綫**) is a [[lilac_light_rail]] route connecting Light Rail Depot stop and Enchanted South Hill stop, and is jointly owned by [[lilypad_rail]] and Qingli Railways.

=====History=====
On 17 September 2022, Qingli Railways proposed this route together with an upgrade on Kallos LRT stop (current Light Rail Depot stop) to a High Speed stop. 

On 10 October 2022, this route is completed with the new Light Rail Depot stop. 

On 29 October 2023, Royal Park is added to Enchanted South Hill bound for future connections to Elizabeth Line and [[lrt_804|route 804]].

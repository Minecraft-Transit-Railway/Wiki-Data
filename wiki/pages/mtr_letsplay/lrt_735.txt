=====Lilac Light Rail Route 735=====
**Lilac Light Rail Route 735** (Chinese: **麗蓮輕鐵735綫**) is a [[lilac_light_rail]] route connecting Tao Tung stop and Sunrise stop, and is jointly owned by [[lilypad_rail]] and [[qingli_railways|Qingli Railways]].

=====History=====
On 9 May 2023, Route 735 was added to the Tempest light rail system project together with [[lrt_736|route 736]]. This route was suggested by [[qingli_railways|Qingli Railways]] to connect Sunrise station to Tao Tung stop through the horizontal diameter in the circle of [[lrt_717_8|route 717/718]]. Apart from the route itself, 4 new stations were planned between Tai Wai stop and Toodeepic Ocean stop.

On 9 July 2023, route 735 is completed and fully operational together with the whole Tempest light rail network. 

On 20 October 2023, [[qingli_railways|Qingli Railways]] proposed the "Lilac Light Rail Reform Plan 2023". According to the plan, Route 735 will be extended to Daimon North stop in order to replace the connection of [[lrt_721_2|route 721/722]] between Toodeepic Ocean, Dynamic, and places in the south of that. The line color of route 735 will also be changed to a darker color as the current color is too similar to that of [[lrt_410|route 410]].

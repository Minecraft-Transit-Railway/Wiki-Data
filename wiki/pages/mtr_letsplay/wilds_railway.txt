**Wilds Railway** <sub>(aka WR, 曠野鐵路)</sub> is a fictional railway company in Let's Play server, and was established on the 8th August 2022. Managed by [[mtr_community:staff:navi_lx9702|Navi]], one of the builders and part of the staff team.

The company is set to be the subsidiary of 'Suzuran Railways', the railway company that operates only in Navi_LX9702's private world.

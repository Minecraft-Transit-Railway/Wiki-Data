======Lilac Light Rail Route 601======
**Lilac Light Rail Route 601** (Chinese: **麗蓮輕鐵601綫**) is a [[lilac_light_rail]] route connecting Jonathan’s Resort stop and Loyalty Hills stop, and is jointly owned by [[lilypad_rail]] and Qingli Railways. This route is proposed in the "Lilac Light Rail Reform Plan 2023", and will be opened in the future.

=====History=====
On 20 October 2023, [[qingli_railways|Qingli Railways]] proposed the "Lilac Light Rail Reform Plan 2023", and route 601 is included in this plan. This route aims to replace [[lrt_882|route 482P]] and the section between Heung Fan Liu Airport South and Jonathan’s Resort of [[lrt_882|route 882]].

According to "Lilac Light Rail Reform Plan 2023", the color of route 601 is tentatively decided to be honey-yellow.

======Lilac Light Rail Route 803======
''This page is introducing an unopened route.''
\\ **Lilac Light Rail Route 803** (Chinese: **麗蓮輕鐵803綫**) is a [[lilac_light_rail]] route connecting Light Rail Depot stop and South Memorial stop, and is jointly owned by [[lilypad_rail]] and [[qingli_railways|Qingli Railways]]. This route is planned on "Lilac Light Rail Route 2023", and will be opened in the future.

=====History=====
On 20 October 2023, [[qingli_railways|Qingli Railways]] proposed the "Lilac Light Rail Reform Plan 2023", and Route 803 is included in this plan. Route 803 aims to connect Makki Memorial Village area to the rest of the light rail system.

On 29 October 2023, Royal Park stop is added to route serving Light Rail Depot stop. Route 803 is announced to be also stopping at this stop when opened.

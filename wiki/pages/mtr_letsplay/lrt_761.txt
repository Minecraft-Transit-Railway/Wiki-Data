======Lilac Light Rail Route 761======
**Lilac Light Rail Route 761** (Chinese: **麗蓮輕鐵761綫**) is a [[lilac_light_rail]] route connecting Tempest stop and Daimon North stop, and is jointly owned by [[lilypad_rail]] and [[qingli_railways|Qingli Railways]].

=====History=====
On 25 January 2023, route 761 was proposed to be the first light rail route connecting Tempest station, replacing the original [[lrt_720|route 720]] plan. This route is planned to be the first route using overhead wire cable instead of third rail electrification in the Qingli Light Rail system.((The first route to use overhead wire cable electrification in the Lilac Light Rail system is [[lrt_505|route 505]].)) The construction commenced on the same day.

On 5 February 2023, route 761 is completed and fully operational.

On 20 October 2023, [[qingli_railways|Qingli Railways]] released the "Lilac Light Rail Reform Plan 2023". According to the plan, route 761 will be merged with [[lrt_736|route 736P]] such that it’s eastern terminus will be moved from Tempest to Tao Tung. Also, route 761 will be further extended from Daimon North to Daimon Island via Ginga, to provide connections to [[kallos_express]], Effirt Express and Llanmara Express.

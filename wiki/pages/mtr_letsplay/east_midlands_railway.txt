**East Midlands Railway** is a train operating company on the Minecraft Transit Railway Let's Play server. It operates services from Llanmara Prince's Street to Liskeard Central via Ilford Island, alongside long-distance and stopping services towards the North-East from Liskeard Central. The operator replaced the now-defunct Electrostar Rail brand. It operates a fleet of fleet of Class 153, 158, 170, 350, 377 and 802 units, additionally providing stock and operating the Magnolia - Pawtecket branch trains on behalf of the [[mtr_letsplay:wilds_railway|Wilds Railway]] & [[mtr_letsplay:lilypad_rail|Lilypad Rail]] Overground TOC.

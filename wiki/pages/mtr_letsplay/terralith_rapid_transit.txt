Terralith Rapid Transit([[wp>Chinese_language|Chinese]]: 泰瑞斯捷運, Common Notation: TRT) is a management company that manages lines and trains such as [[mtr_letsplay:shimanomtr|Shimano MTR]].

======Overview=====
This is a management company ((a company equivalent to a Category-3 Operator (第三種鉄道事業者) in Japan)) that manages lines and trains established by DelphoxOTS when it joined the Let's Play server.
We own almost all [[mtr_letsplay:shimanomtr|Shimano MTR]] and [[mtr_letsplay:shimanolrt|Shimano LRT]] lines and trains. The operation of the trains is outsourced to companies such as [[mtr_letsplay:shimanomtr|Shimano MTR]].

=====Train specifications=====
SMTR is required to install emergency doors at the front of all carriages.
Furthermore, from August 2024 onwards, gangways will, in principle, be required to be designed so that passengers can pass through from front to back.
SLRT is not required to install emergency doors, but the above standards must be adhered to if it provides through service to SMTR (including only entering SMTR at one station).
Currently, only Lines S4 and S11 do not allow intrusion into the driver's cab ((Instead, the hit box has been adjusted to make the wall of the driver's cab less visible.)) This may be expanded to other lines in the future.
Due to concerns about schedule disruption, manual operation will not be implemented except during events.

=====Station Specifications=====
In principle, the following equipment must be installed for SMTR.
  * Platform Doors
  * PID
  * Station Name
  * Exit/Interchange Signs
  * Elevators ((Must be accessible to the exit)) ((Escape call button installed at the bottom))
  * Escalators
  * Stairs ((Must be accessible to the exit))
  * Ticket Gates ((Ticket processors not permitted))
  * Ticket machines installed inside and outside the ticket gates ((Ticket machines inside the ticket gates are for over-ride settlement))
  * Station Announcement Equipment
Each station has a station name and a station number. Stations not managed by TRT generally do not have station numbers, but they are assigned internally. Note that slashes are included, so you will need to exclude them when searching for routes on Discord.

For SLRT, the rules are much more relaxed than above, but they must have at least PID, station name, ticket machine, and ticket processor. Conversely, ticket gates are not permitted.

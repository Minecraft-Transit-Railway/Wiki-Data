=====Kallos Light Rail Route 801X=====
''This page is introducing a discontinued route. For the current route, please visit "[[lrt_802]]"''

**Kallos Light Rail Route 801X** (Chinese: **麗灣輕鐵801X綫**) is a route which runs from Light Rail Depot to Lei Yue Mun, operated by Qingli Railways, serving passengers between Kallos and Toitoi.

====History====
On 3 September, Chin Ka Village LRT (Route 762) completed. Qingli Railways proposed a new through run route running from Light Rail Depot to Toitoi. This route sticks with route 801P (currently [[lrt_802|802]]) till Puketeraki, and goes via Toitoi to Lei Yue Mun. 

On 10 September 2022, route 801P was extended to Puketeraki. This route was opened together.

On 4 December 2022, this route is discontinued, and the connection between Puketeraki and Toitoi is replaced by [[lrt_802|route 802]]. This route serviced 85 days in total.

Before this route is discontinued, it was shown as magenta in the light rail route map, which is the same as the current [[lrt_802|route 802 or 802S]]

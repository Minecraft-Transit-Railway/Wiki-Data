The '''WR Mimura Line ''(Chinese: 曠鐵三村綫)''''' is one of the route that [[/Wilds_Railway|Wilds Railway]] operates. The route connects [[mtr_lp:hamura_station|Hamura Station]] and [[mtr_lp:tai_po_station|Tai Po Station]], acts as one of the route to access Mimura Area and Lebyazhsk. 


The route is also the second route in the [[/Let's_Play_Server|Let's Play Server]] to use London Underground trains, and is the only operational Tube line until [[mtr_lp:metro_devota_city_line|Metro Devota & City Line]] starts it's service, therefore is often rode and seen in Jon's videos.

==History==
The route is planned by Navi in 7 August, 2022, with a initial plan from [[mtr_lp:tai_po_station|Tai Po]] to [[mtr_lp:hamura_station|Hamura]]. The proposal is then posted by Makki means the route is allowed to be built.

==Rolling stock==
The route uses 7 5 cars [[mtr_lp:london_underground_1995_stock|London Underground 1995 stock]] EMUs. The trains are manufactured by [[mtr_lp:hitachi|Hitachi]].

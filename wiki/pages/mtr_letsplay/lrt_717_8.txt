======Lilac Light Rail Route 717/718======
**Lilac Light Rail Route 717/718** (Chinese: **麗蓮輕鐵717/ 718綫**) are 2 circular routes jointly owned by [[qingli_railways|Qingli Railways]] and [[lilypad_rail]], set up in Tempest and Sunrise area. Routes 717 and 718 are actually the same route, but in different directions. Route 717 is in clockwise direction; while Route 718 is in anti-clockwise direction.

=====History=====
On 2 April 2023, [[lilypad_rail]] proposed route 717 going from Tempest to the west and turn north at Boundary Island stop.

On 9 April 2023, route 717 is proposed to further extend to Sunrise and then back to Tempest to form a circular route. Different directions of route 717 has been split into routes 717 and 718.

On 9 July 2023, the loop is completed and routes 717 and 718 has been fully operational since then.

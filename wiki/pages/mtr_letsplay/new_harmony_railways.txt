The '''New Harmony Railways (NHR)'''  is one of the transit operators in the [[/Let’s_Play_Server|Let’s Play Server]], 
established on the 22nd of July 2022, managed by [[mtr_lp:teatea9487_tpwd|TeaTea9487_Tpwd]] (Staff) and [[mtr_users:kettlekettle|kettlekettle]] (Builder). The operator was separated from [[mtr_lp:harmony_rail_transit|Harmony Rail Transit]] (HRT), which was established on June 2021, when [[mtr_lp:minecraft_transit_railway_discord|Minecraft Transit Railway Discord]] put a sanction on it on the 21st of July 2022.

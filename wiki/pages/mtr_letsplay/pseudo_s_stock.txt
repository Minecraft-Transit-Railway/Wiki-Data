**Wilds Railway / Lilypad Rail S4-P and S5-P Stocks** are trains that runs in the Let's Play server. They are based on M-train small variant, with texture that strongly resembles a [[wp>London_Underground_S7_and_S8_Stock|London Underground S7 and S8 Stock]]. The train starts to be in service from November 2022 and still operates in both Victory Line and Aldgate & Resort Line.

===== In Let's Play server =====

==== Wilds Railway S4-P Stock ====

[[mtr_letsplay:wilds_railway]] acquired one S4-P stock from an unknown leasing company [[wp>Porterbook]] and painted the handrail to A&R Green. The train enters service from November 2022 once the line has opened.

==== Lilypad Rail S5-P Stock ====

Following the introduction of S4-P stocks in Aldgate & Resort Line, [[mtr_letsplay:lilypad_rail]] raised an interest of introducing the SX-P stocks to unify the cooperate image in Greater Llanmara. As Lilypad Rail is satisfied by the quality of the train, they introduced a **finite number of** nearly identical train with Wilds Railway S4-P Stock, with the only 2 differences of being 5 cars and have all the handrails painted red instead. They are therefore called S5-P stocks, and entered service in around November to December 2022.

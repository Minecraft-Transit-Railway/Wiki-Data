====== HSR Line Number ======

HSR line number is a number and letter string for high-speed railway numbering. In[[start|Let's Play]]Server，The number is in the following way.

1number+Terminal abbreviation 3 letters+2numbers

For example：[[1lla39|1LLA39]]，[[hsr_north_heights_line|North Heights Line]] to [[llanmara|Llanmara]]，The "LLA" in the middle is the abbreviation of Llanmara.

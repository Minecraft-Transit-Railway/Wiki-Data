====== 齐齐玩服务器手动驾驶指南 ======

作为[[mtr:4.0.x|MTR 4.0更新]]的一部分，手动驾驶系统是[[https://youtu.be/xYEbU6G7jzo|重大更新]]。当Let's Play升级到MTR4时，建筑商能够利用新功能来增强其线路和服务的访客体验。Let's Play现在有许多线路可以由访客手动驱动。以下是可以手动驾驶的线路和服务的列表。

<sup>该指南最初版本由 JamesQQ 编写。</sup>

----

===== 怀旧线 =====

<color white/#25ADF6>**<html>&nbsp怀旧线&nbsp</html>**</color>驾驶难度：3/5，包含了地上、地下车站和路段。

| **手动驾驶车辆数**  | 4 |
| **如何前往车厂**  | 前往灌丛原站，在站内(-4221, 66, 7363)处搭乘APM即可到达。 |
| **限速表** | 300km/h |
| **加速度** | 4.0m/s/s |
| **减速度** | 4.0m/s/s |

关于<color white/#25ADF6>**<html>&nbsp怀旧线&nbsp</html>**</color>的更多信息详见[[mtr_letsplay:nostalgia_line|这里]]。

===== Fryske Sprinter =====

The <color white/#B0B0BE>**<html>&nbspFryske Sprinter&nbsp</html>**</color> is rated 4/5 on the driving difficulty scale. The line features above above ground and surface-tunnel stations, with viaduct, surface level, and surface tunnel driving. The line features a number of stopping pattern variations, but the stopping pattern is not known to the player at the depot. Therefore, the player should be careful of speeding past any one station.

| **手动驾驶车辆数**  | 2 |
| **如何前往车厂**  | 前往鲸鱼湖站，沿着小路走即可到达。 |
| **限速表** |   |
| **加速度** | 1.2m/s/s |
| **减速度** | 1.2m/s/s |

For more information on the <color white/#B0B0BE>**<html>&nbspFryske Sprinter&nbsp</html>**</color>, click [[mtr_letsplay:fryskeline|here]].

**ElectrostarJudge** is a staff member in the [[mtr:start|Minecraft Transit Railway]] Discord community. He joined in December 2021 and became a member of the Staff Team as a Moderator in July 2022, before subsequently being the final person promoted to Staff before the Moderator role was dissolved and merged with the Staff role. Electrostar also owns the namesake operator Electrostar Rail, his first large project, Great Western, a regional suburban project, as well as CrossCountry, an InterCity style service operating across a number of other lines on the [[mtr_letsplay:start|Let's Play Server]] to connect cities in the similar style to the real life [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CrossCountry|CrossCountry Trains]] operator.

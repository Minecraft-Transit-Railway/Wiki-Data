====== Trinsmoton Rail Class 150 Commuter Worker ======

The **Trinsmoton Rail Class 150 Commuter Worker** is a 3rd Rail Electric Multiple Unit built by Movin-Cammel in the 1970s. The train is one of the first trains to use sliding doors, alongside the [[mtr_community:trinsmoton_rail:class_203|Class 203]] and [[mtr_community:trinsmoton_rail:class_146|Class 146 Commuter Worker]] EMU.

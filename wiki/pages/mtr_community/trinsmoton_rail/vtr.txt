{{:mtr_community:trinsmoton_rail:vtr.png?400|}}====== Vexton Transit Railway ======

The **Vexton Transit Railway (VTR)** is a public transport company in Trinsmoton. It consists of 1 line (**centrelink**) and It will continue to expand throughout the whole server.

====== Vexton Transit Railway Crossrail ======

The **Vexton Transit Railway Crossrail** is a company also managed by VTR and is made for crossrail lines.

====== centrelink ======

**centrelink** as of now is not constructed or has began constructed but it is a crossrail line that will run North to South and the branches will split off to East and the West.

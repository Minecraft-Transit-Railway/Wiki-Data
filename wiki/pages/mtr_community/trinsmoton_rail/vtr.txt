====== Vexton Transit Railway ======

The **Vexton Transit Railway (VTR)** is a public transport company in Trinsmoton. It consists of 1 line (**centrelink**) and It will continue to expand throughout the whole county of Trinsmoton.

====== Croydon Railway ======

VTR began its life as **Croydon Railway (CR)**, now a defunct sector which ran from Spawn to Jungle Wood and Ocean Bay to Pentium Hills. Following refurbishment of the company, the routes were operated by [[mtr_community:trinsmoton_rail:northern|Northern Rail]] for few months during VTR's refurbishment.

====== Vexton Transit Railway Crossrail ======

The **VTR Crossrail** is a sector of VTR and connects different places of the Northern Region of Trinsmoton.

====== Centrelink ======

**Centrelink** as of now is not constructed or has began constructed but it is a crossrail line that will run North to South and the branches will split off to East and the West.

====== Sierra High Speed ======
**Sierra High Speed** is a High Speed Railway connecting Snow District and Spawn in fast speeds. It’s very similar to [[mtr_community:trinsmoton_rail:nctr|NCTR]] Lorem Ipsum Line however the Sierra High Speed does not call at any stations along the way to Sierra de Alpino and is fully Underground.

====== NCTR ======

**New Croydon Transit Railway (NCTR) **is a long distance rapid transit system serving mostly the southern region of Trinsmoton. It currently has 1 line open: Lorem Ipsum Line, which takes passengers from Ocean Bay to the Sierra District.

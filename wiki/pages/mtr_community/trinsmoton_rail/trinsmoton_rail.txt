====== Trinsmoton Rail ======

The **Trinsmoton Rail (TR) **is a public transport company in Trinsmoton. It consists of 2 sectors: [[mtr_community:trinsmoton_rail:trinsmoton_rail#Regional|Regional]] and [[mtr_community:trinsmoton_rail:trinsmoton_rail#High Speed|High Speed]]. The company will serve the Eastern Region of Trinsmoton as the other routes around Trinsmoton are currently operated by seperate transport companies.

====== Regional ======

**Regional **is a sector of Trinsmoton Rail, serving all stations around the east Trinsmoton.

====== High Speed ======

**High Speed **is a sector of Trinsmoton Rail, it's a faster varient of [[mtr_community:trinsmoton_rail:trinsmoton_rail#Regional|Regional]] as it doesn't stop at rural areas or villages.

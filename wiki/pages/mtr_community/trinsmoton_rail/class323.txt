====== Trinsmoton Rail Class 323 (BR) ======
The **Trinsmoton Rail Class 323 (BR)** is an electric multiple unit that operates on various routes.
===== Class 323/1 =====
The Class 323/1 is the first version of the series, being built in 1988. The trains looks identical to the British Rail Class 321, 320 and 322s and are used in the regional services.

====== Trinsmoton Rail Class 251 Commutra ======

The **Trinsmoton Rail Class 251** is a Third Rail Electric Multiple Unit from the Commutra Family built for [[mtr_community:trinsmoton_rail:zenrail#Regional|ZenRail Regional]]. The trains currently run on the [[mtr_community:trinsmoton_rail:zenrail#routes|Whitecrest - Cornwall Line & Airport - Cornwall Line]].

On the surface the Class 251 looks very much alike to the [[mtr_community:trinsmoton_rail:class_250|Class 250 Commutra]], however the headlights looks a bit different and the in train Display Boards uses LED screens similar to the [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alstom_Aventra|British Rail Alstom Aventras]].

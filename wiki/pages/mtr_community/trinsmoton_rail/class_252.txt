====== Trinsmoton Rail Class 252 Commutra ======

The **Trinsmoton Rail Class 252 Super Commutra** is a proposed Bi-Mode Electric Multiple Unit that is designed to run on Intercity services. The trains consist of 3-8 carriages and can be coupled up with the [[mtr_community:trinsmoton_rail:class_250|Class 250]] Commutras. They will serve the High Speed Sector of Trinsmoton Rail.

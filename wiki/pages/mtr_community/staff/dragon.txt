**Aecth** (/æçθ/, **Aec**-ith), Also known as **Dragon**, Is a Resource Pack Developer known for his Medium-Quality Models. He Originates from the United states of America. He is known for being a Fan of [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F-Zero|F-Zero]] and the [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wings_of_Fire_(novel_series)|Wings of Fire Book Series]].

===== The PäRail Trains Addon =====

The [[mtr_extra:parail_trains|PäRail Pack]]((Known Shortly as the PRL Pack, Or Officially as the PäRail Trains Addon)) is Aecth's Main Project back in October 2022.

===== Outside MTR =====

Dragon is Mainly known outside of MTR For his Art and Love of the Wings of Fire book Series. He also has an Interest in the F-ZERO Series of Games. He also is an Artist, mainly with Drawing Dragons.

===== History In Community =====

**2021**
Dragon joined the MTR Community around May 2021, Most intonation from this era is lost, But remaining posts show the beginning of an early version of PCMA.

**2022**
Along with the previous era, Information from 2022 is Limited. What's known is that he help create the Innova APM 100 Resource Pack, And released early versions of the PCMA Resource pack and world.

**Early 2023**
This is the Largest Part of Dragon's History, Including his Brief Stint on [[mtr_letsplay:start|the Let's Play Server]] with the West Coast Railroad (Demolished as of Season 3). He Restarted development of the PCMA World, Twice. He also renamed the Project to "6 Cities Transportation" This is also where he started Development of the PäRail Pack.

**Late 2023 to 2025**
Here, He returned to The MTR Staff team as part of the Support Team.

**2025 and Beyond**
Currently, Aecth is applying final touches to the 6 Cities Transportation World

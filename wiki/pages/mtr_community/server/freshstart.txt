==== Kapple Transport Agency ====
Kapple Transport Agency (KTA) is a company split from [[mtr:mtr_community:server:freshstart#Companies#National Rail|National Rail]]. It controls most lines in the Kapple Area, with the exception being South Main Line, which does not serve the Kapple area.

===== Info =====

The [[mtr_community:centralia:rcc|Rhosyngardd City Council]] is a Open Access Rail Operator only operating trains for tourism. Their lines typically branch out from [[mtr_community:centralia:stations:rhosyngarddcentral|Rhosyngardd Central]]. [[mtr_community:centralia:rcc|RCC]] is known for being the only ones to have put up a serious restriction in the world, which is to keep trains from entering the [[mtr_community:centralia:buses:cityofrhosyngardd|Rhosyngardd City]] walls at all costs. They used to operate light rail in the [[mtr_community:centralia:stations:hibiki|Hibiki]] area, but after RCC Tramways merged with AIRL to create the [[mtr_community:centralia:centrewest|Centrewest]] network, all that remains is a few B90s with [[mtr_community:centralia:rcc|RCC]] livery on the [[mtr_community:centralia:centrewest:greenline|Centrewest Green Line]].
